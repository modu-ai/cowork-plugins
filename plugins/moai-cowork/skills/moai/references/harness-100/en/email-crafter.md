# Email Crafter (harness-100-email-crafter)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
The Email Crafter harness empowers marketers, customer success teams, and business professionals to design and execute high-performing email campaigns. From subject line optimization and body copy writing to A/B testing strategies and deliverability best practices, this harness provides proven frameworks for creating emails that engage, convert, and delight. Ideal for anyone sending customer communications, marketing campaigns, or newsletters seeking to maximize open rates, click rates, and customer satisfaction.

## Expert Roles
**1. Subject Line Strategist**
Crafts compelling subject lines that drive opens. Responsibilities: research audience preferences and testing data, write A/B test variations, optimize for preview text, ensure mobile readability, balance personalization and compliance.

**2. Body Copy Specialist**
Writes persuasive email body content that drives desired action. Responsibilities: write benefit-driven copy, design email hierarchy and flow, craft compelling CTAs, incorporate social proof, maintain brand voice consistency.

**3. Campaign Architect**
Designs complete email campaign sequences and strategies. Responsibilities: define campaign goals and success metrics, create segmentation and audience targeting, design email sequences, establish send schedules, plan measurement approach.

**4. A/B Testing Manager**
Designs rigorous testing frameworks to optimize email performance. Responsibilities: identify testable variables, design test structures and sample sizes, analyze statistical significance, recommend optimizations, maintain testing roadmap.

**5. Deliverability Specialist**
Ensures emails reach inboxes and maintain sender reputation. Responsibilities: monitor sender reputation and authentication, optimize list hygiene, manage unsubscribe and complaint rates, troubleshoot deliverability issues, ensure legal compliance.

## Workflow
### Phase 1: Context Gathering
- Define campaign goals, audience, and success metrics
- Analyze historical email performance and benchmarks
- Identify segmentation criteria and audience preferences
- Assess compliance requirements and deliverability constraints
- Gather brand guidelines and messaging frameworks

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production
- Research and test subject line variations
- Write compelling body copy and design hierarchy
- Create email template and responsive design
- Develop A/B testing strategy and sample sizes
- Establish segmentation, timing, and sending sequences

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
- Validate design across email clients and devices
- Audit for compliance and deliverability issues
- Create measurement dashboard and KPI tracking
- Prepare campaign launch checklist and procedures
- Document lessons learned and optimization recommendations

## Deliverables
- **Email Template Design:** Responsive HTML template with component library
- **Subject Line Variations:** Multiple A/B test options with performance predictions
- **Campaign Copy:** Complete email body with CTAs, social proof, and personalization
- **A/B Testing Plan:** Variables, sample sizes, and statistical analysis framework
- **Deliverability Audit:** Authentication setup, list quality, and compliance checklist

## Extension Skills
- **Dynamic Content Personalization:** Design variable content blocks by segment
- **Email Automation Sequences:** Build triggered and workflow-based email programs
- **Mobile & Accessibility Optimization:** Ensure accessibility for all users and devices

## Error Handling
| Error | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Low Open Rates | Weak subject lines or poor sender reputation | Test subject variations, improve list quality, rebuild reputation |
| Low Click Rates | Unclear CTA or misaligned messaging | Simplify design, strengthen value proposition, increase CTA prominence |
| Deliverability Issues | Authentication problems or list quality | Set up SPF/DKIM/DMARC, audit and clean lists, consult ISP deliverability experts |
| Compliance Violations | Missing disclosures or unsubscribe options | Review CAN-SPAM and GDPR requirements, audit template, add required elements |
