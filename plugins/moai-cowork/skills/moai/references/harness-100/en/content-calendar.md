# Content Calendar (harness-content-calendar)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
Content Calendar guides marketing, social media, and communications teams through systematic editorial planning and scheduling. This harness helps create themed content, coordinate publishing schedules, align cross-platform messaging, manage seasonal campaigns, and maintain consistent audience engagement across all channels.

## Expert Roles

- **Content Strategy Lead**: Develops overarching content themes aligned with business goals and audience needs
  - **Audience Need Mapping**: Documents audience pain points, questions, aspirations, and content consumption preferences
  - **Content Pillar Definition**: Establishes 3-5 core content themes (education, inspiration, product, community, entertainment)
  - **Campaign Planning**: Creates themed content collections around product launches, seasonal events, and strategic priorities
  - **Messaging Alignment**: Ensures all content reinforces key brand messages and value propositions
  - **Success Metrics**: Defines engagement, reach, and conversion targets for content performance tracking

- **Editorial Calendar Manager**: Orchestrates publishing schedule across channels ensuring consistency and timeliness
  - **Timeline Development**: Maps content delivery calendar 3-6 months forward with key dates, holidays, and campaign milestones
  - **Channel Coordination**: Specifies which content publishes to which channels (blog, social, email, video, podcast) and timing
  - **Content Batching**: Groups related content by theme and sequences for cumulative impact and team efficiency
  - **Contingency Planning**: Maintains buffer content for seasonal/trending topics and quick-response opportunities
  - **Stakeholder Communication**: Confirms content schedule with product, customer success, and business development teams

- **Content Creation Lead**: Oversees production of diverse content formats aligned to strategy and audience needs
  - **Format Strategy**: Determines optimal content mix (blog posts, videos, infographics, case studies, webinars, podcasts)
  - **Production Planning**: Estimates creation timelines and resource needs for each content type
  - **Creator Assignment**: Matches content topics to subject matter experts; coordinates with freelancers or agencies as needed
  - **Quality Standards**: Establishes editorial guidelines, fact-checking, and review processes
  - **Repurposing Strategy**: Plans how core content gets repurposed across formats and channels for maximum reach

- **Cross-Platform Coordinator**: Ensures cohesive messaging and optimized delivery across all customer touchpoints
  - **Platform-Specific Adaptation**: Tailors core message to unique requirements of each channel (Twitter length, LinkedIn tone, YouTube format)
  - **Audience Segmentation**: Directs content to appropriate audience segments via email, social, and site personalization
  - **Link Ecosystem**: Creates logical connections between related content across channels, reducing friction
  - **Comment & Engagement**: Plans response templates and engagement strategies for each platform
  - **Metadata Optimization**: Develops titles, descriptions, hashtags, and keywords for discoverability

- **Campaign & Seasonal Manager**: Plans and executes themed content campaigns aligned to business calendar
  - **Campaign Mapping**: Identifies annual campaign opportunities (product launches, holidays, awareness months, industry events)
  - **Content Narrative**: Develops overarching narrative and specific content pieces for each campaign phase
  - **Cross-channel Sequencing**: Choreographs when content appears on each channel to build momentum (sneak peek, announcement, education, follow-up)
  - **Promotion Planning**: Designs paid and organic promotion to amplify campaign reach and engagement
  - **Performance Analysis**: Measures campaign effectiveness and documents learnings for future optimization

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Context Gathering
1. **Define Content Goals**: Clarify what content should achieve (awareness, education, lead generation, retention, advocacy)
2. **Audience Research**: Document audience demographics, pain points, information sources, and preferred content formats
3. **Competitor Content Audit**: Review competitor content strategy, gaps, and opportunities for differentiation
4. **Available Resources**: Inventory current content creation capacity (in-house team, freelancers, agencies, SMEs)
5. **Platform Analysis**: Document where target audience spends time, algorithm preferences, and engagement patterns

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production

| Order | Task | Owner | Depends On | Deliverable |
|-------|------|-------|------------|-------------|
| 1 | Develop content strategy | Strategy Lead | Goals + Audience | `content-strategy-framework.md` |
| 2 | Create editorial calendar | Calendar Manager | Strategy | `editorial-calendar.xlsx` |
| 3 | Plan campaigns and themes | Campaign Manager | Strategy + Calendar | `campaign-content-plan.md` |
| 4 | Define creation workflows | Creation Lead | Strategy | `content-production-guide.md` |
| 5 | Map cross-platform adaptation | Platform Coordinator | Calendar | `channel-adaptation-guidelines.md` |
| 6 | Schedule content & promotion | Calendar Manager | All Above | `content-publishing-schedule.xlsx` |
| 7 | Create content templates | Creation Lead | Production Guide | `content-templates-library.md` |

**Inter-agent Communication**: Strategy Lead confirms campaign themes with Campaign Manager; Calendar Manager coordinates timing with Creation Lead; Platform Coordinator ensures adaptation guidelines support content creation; Campaign Manager provides performance targets to Calendar Manager.

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
1. **Stakeholder Alignment**: Present calendar to leadership, product, and customer success teams for input and commitment
2. **Resource Validation**: Confirm team capacity can deliver planned content volume; adjust scope if needed
3. **Contingency Planning**: Establish backup content options for trending topics and rapid response opportunities
4. **Promotion Coordination**: Align content promotion (paid, email, social sharing) with publishing schedule
5. **Monitoring Setup**: Establish process for content performance tracking, optimization, and calendar updates

## Deliverables
- **content-strategy-framework.md**: Audience needs, content pillars, campaign themes, and success metrics
- **editorial-calendar.xlsx**: 6-month publishing calendar with dates, titles, formats, channels, and owners
- **campaign-content-plan.md**: Themed content plans for major campaigns with narrative arc and content pieces
- **content-production-guide.md**: Format standards, production timelines, creation workflows, and quality checkpoints
- **channel-adaptation-guidelines.md**: Platform-specific guidelines for adapting core content across channels
- **content-publishing-schedule.xlsx**: Detailed schedule showing each piece of content, publish date, channel, and promotion plan
- **content-templates-library.md**: Pre-built templates for blog posts, social updates, email, and other formats

## Extension Skills
- **SEO Content Strategy**: Optimizes content strategy and calendar for search visibility and organic traffic
- **Audience Growth Strategy**: Plans content specifically designed to attract new audiences and expand reach
- **Performance Dashboard**: Establishes metrics dashboard for real-time tracking of content performance across channels

## Error Handling

| Error Type | Strategy |
|-----------|----------|
| Insufficient Content Capacity | Reduce publishing frequency, repurpose existing content more aggressively, or bring on freelancers/agencies |
| Low Engagement Rates | Audit audience preference; test different formats, topics, and posting times; survey audience for topic requests |
| Inconsistent Platform Performance | Double down on highest-performing channels; test content variations to understand platform algorithms |
| Campaign Theme Irrelevance | Solicit customer feedback; validate theme appeal before full calendar commitment; maintain flexibility to pivot |
| Seasonal Content Misalignment | Build longer lead time for seasonal content; create flexible templates adaptable to different calendar positions |
| Cross-channel Message Breakdown | Create platform-specific review process; use shared asset management system; establish clear adaptation guidelines |
