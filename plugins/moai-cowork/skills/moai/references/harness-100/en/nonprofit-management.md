# Nonprofit Management (harness-100-nonprofit)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
Nonprofit Management is a comprehensive framework for stewarding mission-driven organizations through grant funding, donor cultivation, program impact measurement, board governance, and volunteer coordination. This harness guides non-technical users through the full nonprofit lifecycle without requiring professional grant-writing or nonprofit accounting certifications.

## Expert Roles

**Grant Writing & Funding Manager**
- Identify funding opportunities (government grants, foundations, corporate giving)
- Research funder priorities and alignment with organizational mission
- Develop grant proposals with compelling narratives and clear impact metrics
- Manage grant deadlines, application requirements, and documentation
- Track grant awards, compliance requirements, and reporting obligations

**Donor Relationship Manager**
- Build and segment donor database by giving level, interest, capacity
- Design stewardship strategies (recognition, impact reporting, engagement events)
- Conduct prospect research and personalized cultivation campaigns
- Manage major gift proposals and negotiation of multi-year commitments
- Coordinate donor recognition programs and acknowledgment processes

**Program Impact Measurement Specialist**
- Define program outcomes and align metrics to organizational mission
- Design data collection procedures and evaluation methodologies
- Implement monitoring dashboards tracking beneficiaries and outcome achievement
- Conduct annual impact assessments and program effectiveness reviews
- Prepare impact reports for funders, boards, and public communication

**Board Governance Administrator**
- Manage board meetings, agendas, minutes, and information packages
- Document governance policies (conflict of interest, executive sessions, voting)
- Coordinate board committee work and track committee deliverables
- Facilitate board evaluation and director performance assessment
- Maintain compliance with nonprofit regulations and filing requirements

**Volunteer Coordinator & Community Builder**
- Recruit, screen, and onboard volunteers aligned to skill needs and mission
- Design volunteer job descriptions and role placement strategy
- Create volunteer training and engagement programs
- Track volunteer hours, satisfaction, and retention metrics
- Plan volunteer recognition events and appreciation programs

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Context Gathering
- Assess organizational capacity and resource constraints
- Document current funding streams and revenue diversification needs
- Audit donor database and identify engagement opportunities
- Review prior program evaluation data and impact measurement capability
- Identify board gaps and skill requirements for strategic objectives

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production
- Develop funding diversification strategy and grant prospect pipeline
- Write grant proposals with strong needs statements and evaluation plans
- Design donor engagement campaigns with personalized messaging
- Establish program evaluation framework and impact measurement procedures
- Facilitate board strategic planning and governance policy development

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
- Submit grant applications and manage post-award compliance
- Launch donor cultivation campaigns and track engagement metrics
- Generate annual impact reports and program evaluations
- Conduct board meetings and governance reviews
- Report on volunteer recruitment and engagement outcomes

## Deliverables
- Grant Funding Pipeline & Prospect List (funding opportunities, alignment, timeline)
- Grant Proposal(s) (needs statement, program description, evaluation, budget, sustainability)
- Donor Engagement Strategy (cultivation plan, stewardship approach, recognition structure)
- Program Evaluation Framework (outcomes, metrics, data collection, reporting)
- Board Governance Manual (policies, procedures, roles, compliance checklist)
- Annual Impact Report (program outcomes, beneficiary stories, financial summary)
- Volunteer Recruitment & Management Plan (job descriptions, training, recognition)

## Extension Skills
- Social return on investment (SROI) and cost-effectiveness analysis
- Nonprofit financial sustainability and earned income strategies
- Strategic planning and theory of change development

## Error Handling
- **Grant rejection**: Obtain reviewer feedback; revise proposal based on comments; identify alternative funders with better fit
- **Donor data inaccuracy**: Establish CRM procedures; validate donor information at engagement; correct errors promptly
- **Program outcome underperformance**: Analyze implementation barriers; adjust program design if needed; increase participant support
- **Board governance gaps**: Develop written governance policies; conduct board training; document compliance with nonprofit regulations
- **Volunteer retention issues**: Conduct exit interviews; improve volunteer experience; provide meaningful roles and recognition
- **Impact measurement burden**: Start with core outcomes only; automate data collection where possible; use simple metrics and reporting
