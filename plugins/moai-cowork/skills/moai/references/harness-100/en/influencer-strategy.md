# Influencer Strategy (harness-100-influence)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
Influencer Strategy is a comprehensive framework for identifying, vetting, and managing influencer partnerships to amplify brand reach and drive measurable engagement. This harness guides non-technical users through influencer discovery, contract negotiation, campaign creative development, performance tracking, and ROI analysis without requiring social media expertise or agency support.

## Expert Roles

**Influencer Sourcing Manager**
- Identify and profile potential influencers aligned to brand values and target audience
- Analyze audience demographics, engagement rates, and authenticity metrics
- Build influencer database with contact information and performance history
- Negotiate rates and partnership terms with talent managers and agents
- Maintain relationship pipeline for recurring and seasonal campaigns

**Campaign Brief Developer**
- Translate brand objectives into clear, creative influencer briefs
- Define campaign themes, content requirements, and usage rights
- Establish posting timelines, content approval workflows, and revision procedures
- Specify deliverables (posts, stories, reels, live sessions, long-form content)
- Document brand guidelines and compliance requirements

**Contract & Compliance Specialist**
- Draft influencer talent agreements covering fees, deliverables, exclusivity
- Ensure FTC/ASA disclosure compliance (hashtags: #ad, #sponsored)
- Establish intellectual property rights and content reuse agreements
- Manage exclusivity periods and non-compete clauses
- Document compliance documentation and audit trails

**Creative & Performance Analyst**
- Develop creative concepts and messaging frameworks with influencers
- Monitor real-time campaign performance (impressions, engagement, reach)
- Conduct A/B testing of content variations and posting strategies
- Track conversion metrics (clicks, signups, sales, attributed revenue)
- Generate performance reports with insights and optimization recommendations

**ROI & Partnership Evaluator**
- Calculate cost per engagement, cost per conversion, and return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Compare influencer performance against industry benchmarks
- Conduct lifetime value analysis of influencer-acquired customers
- Evaluate brand sentiment and audience quality improvements
- Recommend influencer partnerships for renewal or sunsetting

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Context Gathering
- Define campaign objectives (awareness, consideration, conversion, retention)
- Identify target audience demographics and psychographics
- Establish budget allocation across influencer tiers (mega, macro, micro, nano)
- Review competitive influencer landscape and best practices
- Select social platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn) and content formats

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production
- Source and vet influencer candidates using discovery tools and platforms
- Evaluate audience authenticity and engagement quality
- Develop customized campaign briefs aligned to influencer creative style
- Negotiate partnership terms and contract agreements
- Launch campaigns with clear approval workflows and performance tracking

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
- Monitor real-time performance metrics and identify content winners
- Conduct audience sentiment analysis and brand lift measurement
- Calculate total ROI and cost per acquisition metrics
- Compile case studies and performance documentation
- Plan next campaign iterations based on learnings and optimization insights

## Deliverables
- Influencer Sourcing Report (candidate profiles, audience analysis, recommendation)
- Campaign Brief Document (objectives, messaging, deliverables, timeline)
- Influencer Talent Agreement (fees, terms, IP rights, compliance clauses)
- Creative Concept Deck (content themes, visual direction, messaging frameworks)
- Performance Dashboard (real-time metrics, engagement, reach, conversion tracking)
- ROI Analysis & Case Study (cost per conversion, ROAS, audience insights)
- Compliance Audit Report (FTC/ASA disclosure verification, documentation)

## Extension Skills
- Micro-influencer community building and long-term ambassador programs
- User-generated content amplification and hashtag campaign management
- Crisis communication and influencer reputation risk management

## Error Handling
- **Audience authenticity issues**: Use third-party verification tools; request analytics access; prioritize engagement rate over follower count
- **Brand misalignment**: Conduct cultural fit assessment beyond follower demographics; review influencer's prior brand partnerships
- **Non-compliance with regulations**: Establish compliance checklist for all influencers; include mandatory #ad disclosure language in contracts
- **Non-delivery of content**: Include content approval timelines and revision limits in contracts; establish payment holdback pending delivery
- **Negative influencer behavior**: Conduct ongoing reputation monitoring; include behavior clauses and termination rights in contracts
- **Poor attribution**: Use unique discount codes, landing pages, or UTM parameters for each influencer; implement conversion tracking infrastructure
