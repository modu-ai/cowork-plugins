# Copywriting (harness-100-copywriting)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
The Copywriting harness empowers marketers, entrepreneurs, and content creators to craft persuasive, high-converting copy for digital and print campaigns. Whether you're writing website headlines, email subject lines, product descriptions, or ad copy, this harness guides you through proven persuasion frameworks and audience psychology to maximize engagement and conversion rates. Ideal for anyone launching marketing campaigns or refining brand messaging.

## Expert Roles
**1. Conversion Copywriter**
Specializes in high-performing sales copy that drives measurable results. Responsibilities: craft magnetic headlines, write benefit-driven body copy, design compelling calls-to-action, optimize for A/B testing, ensure compliance with platform guidelines.

**2. Email Campaign Specialist**
Masters the art of inbox-opening subject lines and retention messaging. Responsibilities: develop segmented email sequences, write welcome series templates, design re-engagement campaigns, calculate send-time optimization, analyze performance metrics.

**3. Product Storyteller**
Transforms features into emotional narratives that resonate with buyers. Responsibilities: craft origin and value stories, highlight unique selling propositions, write customer pain-point solutions, develop testimonial narratives, create FAQ content.

**4. Ad Copy Strategist**
Optimizes messaging for paid social, search, and display advertising. Responsibilities: write platform-specific ad variations, condense messaging for character limits, design attention-grabbing hooks, calculate cost-per-click predictions, manage ad copy testing frameworks.

**5. Landing Page Architect**
Designs persuasive page structures that guide visitors toward conversion. Responsibilities: organize information hierarchy, write above-the-fold copy, design headline sequences, craft objection-handling sections, write trust-building statements.

## Workflow
### Phase 1: Context Gathering
- Identify target audience, buyer persona, and psychographics
- Define campaign goal, conversion metric, and success criteria
- Analyze competitor messaging and positioning
- Gather brand voice guidelines, tone preferences, and compliance rules

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production
- Map customer pain points and desired outcomes
- Research emotional triggers and persuasion principles
- Draft multiple copy variations using proven frameworks
- Test messaging for clarity, credibility, and compliance

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
- Validate copy against brand guidelines and legal requirements
- Create A/B testing recommendations and performance benchmarks
- Package copy for immediate deployment across channels
- Document performance tracking and iteration guidelines

## Deliverables
- **Headline Variations (5+):** Multiple versions optimized for platform and audience
- **Email Campaign Suite:** Subject lines, preview text, body copy, and CTA sequences
- **Product Description:** Feature-to-benefit translation with use cases and edge cases
- **Ad Copy (Platform-Specific):** Google Ads, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook variations
- **Landing Page Script:** Hero section, benefit sections, objection handling, trust builders

## Extension Skills
- **Tone & Voice Refinement:** Match copy to brand personality across channels
- **A/B Testing Plan:** Design copy variations and measurement frameworks
- **Localization & Adaptation:** Translate copy while preserving persuasive intent

## Error Handling
| Error | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Generic/Uninspired Copy | Insufficient audience research | Deepen persona analysis, conduct interviews, test messaging |
| Compliance Violation | Unaware of platform/legal rules | Review FTC guidelines, platform policies, legal restrictions |
| Low Engagement | Weak headline or value prop | Test emotional hooks, simplify messaging, emphasize urgency/scarcity |
| Misaligned Tone | Disconnect with brand voice | Audit existing messaging, realign to brand guidelines, resample tone |
