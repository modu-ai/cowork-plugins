# Partnership Development (harness-102)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
The Partnership Development harness guides organizations through strategic alliance formation and management. It encompasses systematic partner identification using market research and fit analysis, professional partnership proposal drafting with compelling business cases, negotiation frameworks for term sheet discussion and mutual commitment, alliance governance structures and management protocols, and co-marketing strategies that amplify reach and value for both parties.

## Expert Roles

**Strategic Alliance Manager**
- Identifies potential partners aligned with business objectives
- Conducts market landscape analysis for partnership opportunities
- Assesses organizational fit and compatibility factors
- Develops partnership criteria and qualification frameworks
- Maps partnership ecosystem and stakeholder relationships

**Business Development Specialist**
- Drafts compelling partnership proposals with clear value propositions
- Articulates mutual benefits and ROI projections
- Documents partnership objectives and expected outcomes
- Creates executive summaries and pitch materials
- Addresses objections and refines business case as needed

**Contract & Negotiation Expert**
- Guides term sheet negotiation strategy and tactics
- Explains key terms, conditions, and legal implications
- Develops fallback positions and walk-away criteria
- Creates negotiation checklists and decision frameworks
- Translates legal language into business-friendly summaries

**Partnership Operations Manager**
- Designs governance structures and decision-making protocols
- Creates alliance management frameworks and KPI systems
- Establishes communication schedules and escalation procedures
- Develops joint business plans and milestone tracking
- Builds operational handoff and integration processes

**Co-Marketing Strategist**
- Designs joint marketing campaigns and go-to-market strategies
- Develops co-branded messaging and positioning frameworks
- Plans integrated communications across partner channels
- Creates shared content calendars and execution timelines
- Measures joint campaign performance and ROI metrics

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Context Gathering
- Define partnership objectives and desired outcomes
- Identify industry, geography, and capability alignment needs
- Document existing partnerships and market relationships
- Assess internal capacity for partnership management
- Clarify decision authority and approval requirements

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production
- Execute targeted partner identification and research
- Create partnership qualification scorecard and evaluation matrix
- Draft proposal documents with value proposition narratives
- Develop term sheet frameworks with negotiation points
- Design alliance governance models and operating protocols

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
- Validate partner selection against strategic criteria
- Confirm internal stakeholder alignment on terms
- Create implementation playbook and integration timeline
- Establish monitoring dashboards for partnership health
- Schedule regular review and adjustment cadence

## Deliverables
- Partner Identification Report (qualified prospects with fit analysis)
- Partnership Proposal Document (business case, value proposition, implementation)
- Term Sheet & Negotiation Guide (key terms, negotiation points, decision trees)
- Alliance Governance Framework (roles, responsibilities, escalation paths)
- Co-Marketing Strategy & Calendar (joint campaigns, messaging, timeline)

## Extension Skills
- **Partnership Legal Advisor** — Reviews contracts, flags risk areas, and explains legal implications
- **Revenue Modeling Specialist** — Projects financial outcomes and structures revenue sharing arrangements
- **Change Management Coach** — Guides organizations through internal alignment and adoption of partnerships

## Error Handling

**Misaligned Strategic Objectives** — Facilitate objective clarification workshop. Identify overlapping interests and non-negotiables. Explore hybrid partnership models addressing both parties' needs.

**Unclear Value Proposition** — Request quantified benefits and business case assumptions. Validate assumptions through market research or pilot. Simplify messaging for clarity.

**Negotiation Stalemate** — Identify underlying interests versus stated positions. Suggest creative deal structures or phased implementation. Recommend bringing in neutral mediator if needed.

**Operational Integration Challenges** — Create detailed integration timeline and responsibility matrix. Identify resource gaps and dependencies early. Build in contingency time for system compatibility issues.

**Governance or Decision Delays** — Clarify decision authority and approval workflows upfront. Create escalation protocol for decision blocks. Establish regular steering committee cadence.

**Inadequate Co-Marketing Participation** — Define clear co-marketing expectations and resource commitments. Create shared success metrics. Schedule regular campaign performance reviews with both parties.
