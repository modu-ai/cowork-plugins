# Business Model Canvas (harness-business-model-canvas)

> MoAI-Cowork V.0.1.3 Harness Reference

## Overview
Business Model Canvas guides entrepreneurs, product managers, and business strategists through systematic design of sustainable business models. This harness helps define value propositions, map customer segments, design revenue streams, identify key partnerships, and analyze cost structures using the validated Business Model Canvas framework.

## Expert Roles

- **Value Proposition Designer**: Defines customer problems solved and competitive advantages delivered
  - **Customer Problem Mapping**: Identifies specific customer jobs, pains, and desired outcomes in target segment
  - **Solution Design**: Maps products/services, features, and benefits to each customer need
  - **Differentiation Strategy**: Articulates what makes value proposition unique vs. alternatives
  - **Price-Value Alignment**: Ensures perceived value justifies positioning and price point
  - **Messaging Validation**: Tests value proposition with customers; iterates based on feedback

- **Customer Segment Mapper**: Identifies and prioritizes market targets for maximum impact and feasibility
  - **Market Segmentation**: Divides addressable market by geography, demographics, psychographics, and behavior
  - **Segment Prioritization**: Ranks segments by size, growth, margin potential, and strategic fit
  - **Customer Journey Mapping**: Documents how each segment discovers, evaluates, purchases, and uses offering
  - **Willingness-to-Pay Analysis**: Researches price sensitivity and value perception by segment
  - **Expansion Planning**: Identifies adjacent segments for future growth after initial market validation

- **Revenue Model Architect**: Structures sustainable monetization aligned with customer value and segment economics
  - **Revenue Stream Identification**: Catalogs pricing models (per-unit, subscription, freemium, usage-based, marketplace commission)
  - **Pricing Strategy**: Calculates unit economics, margin targets, and pricing relative to customer willingness-to-pay
  - **Revenue Forecasting**: Projects revenue by segment, product, and customer lifecycle stage
  - **Churn & Retention Analysis**: Models customer lifetime value accounting for acquisition cost and retention rates
  - **Revenue Diversification**: Identifies cross-sell, upsell, and new revenue stream opportunities

- **Key Partnership Strategist**: Designs collaborative relationships that extend capabilities and reduce risk
  - **Partner Identification**: Maps potential partners who reach customer segments, add capability, or reduce costs
  - **Partnership Type Definition**: Clarifies alliance type (distribution, co-development, supply, technology integration)
  - **Interdependency Analysis**: Documents mutual value creation and resource sharing in each partnership
  - **Negotiation Roadmap**: Outlines partner selection criteria, negotiation leverage, and incentive alignment
  - **Ecosystem Mapping**: Places partnerships in broader ecosystem context showing value flow and dependencies

- **Cost Structure Analyst**: Quantifies resource requirements and cost drivers for profitability planning
  - **Cost Categorization**: Catalogs fixed costs (infrastructure, salaries), variable costs (COGS, delivery), and semi-fixed costs
  - **Cost Driver Identification**: Links major cost categories to volume, complexity, or quality decisions
  - **Benchmark Analysis**: Compares cost structure to industry competitors and models improvement opportunities
  - **Break-even Analysis**: Calculates breakeven volume, customer count, and timeline to profitability
  - **Scaling Cost Modeling**: Projects how cost structure changes as business scales (economies/diseconomies of scale)

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Context Gathering
1. **Define Business Concept**: Clarify target customer, problem being solved, and initial value hypothesis
2. **Market Research**: Gather data on target segment size, growth, purchasing power, and competitive landscape
3. **Customer Interviews**: Conduct conversations with potential customers to validate problem and solution approach
4. **Internal Alignment**: Confirm organizational strategy, resources, and go-to-market constraints with leadership
5. **Competitive Landscape**: Document direct and indirect competitors with their value propositions and business models

### Phase 2: Analysis & Production

| Order | Task | Owner | Depends On | Deliverable |
|-------|------|-------|------------|-------------|
| 1 | Design value proposition | Value Designer | Business Concept | `value-proposition-map.md` |
| 2 | Map customer segments | Segment Mapper | Business Concept | `customer-segments-analysis.xlsx` |
| 3 | Define revenue model | Revenue Architect | Value Prop + Segments | `revenue-model-structure.md` |
| 4 | Identify key partners | Partnership Strategist | Value Prop + Segments | `key-partnerships-map.md` |
| 5 | Estimate cost structure | Cost Analyst | Revenue Model | `cost-structure-model.xlsx` |
| 6 | Build financial projections | Revenue Architect | All Above | `financial-projections.xlsx` |
| 7 | Synthesize business model canvas | Segment Mapper | All Above | `business-model-canvas.md` |

**Inter-agent Communication**: Value Designer confirms segment needs alignment with Segment Mapper; Revenue Architect validates pricing feasibility with Cost Analyst; Partnership Strategist identifies cost-sharing opportunities with Cost Analyst; all roles coordinate on financial projections.

### Phase 3: Review & Delivery
1. **Coherence Check**: Validate that all nine canvas elements align and support each other logically
2. **Market Validation**: Confirm customer needs, willingness-to-pay, and willingness-to-recommend through additional customer conversations
3. **Financial Viability**: Stress-test unit economics and breakeven timeline across different growth scenarios
4. **Risk Assessment**: Identify key assumptions with highest impact and lowest confidence; plan validation experiments
5. **Strategy Alignment**: Ensure business model aligns with organizational capabilities, resources, and strategic priorities

## Deliverables
- **value-proposition-map.md**: Customer jobs/pains/gains and how offering addresses each dimension
- **customer-segments-analysis.xlsx**: Segment profiles, sizing, growth rates, and relative prioritization
- **revenue-model-structure.md**: Revenue streams, pricing strategy, and customer lifetime value models
- **key-partnerships-map.md**: Partner types, value exchange, and integration/dependency assumptions
- **cost-structure-model.xlsx**: Fixed, variable, and semi-fixed costs with scaling behavior assumptions
- **financial-projections.xlsx**: 3-year revenue, margin, and breakeven projections by scenario
- **business-model-canvas.md**: One-page visual summary of all nine canvas elements

## Extension Skills
- **Pivot Analysis**: Evaluates alternative business models (different segments, revenue streams, partnerships)
- **Unit Economics Deep Dive**: Calculates detailed CAC, LTV, payback period, and gross margin by segment
- **Growth Strategy**: Models expansion pathways and new market entry strategies building on initial business model

## Error Handling

| Error Type | Strategy |
|-----------|----------|
| Value Proposition Disconnect | Return to customer interviews; test hypothesis with additional customer research; revise based on feedback |
| Segment Priority Misalignment | Model revenue and profit potential by segment; validate market size assumptions through primary research |
| Unsustainable Unit Economics | Reduce COGS through partnerships or efficiency; raise prices if willingness-to-pay supports; shrink addressable market |
| Partnership Feasibility Risk | Identify alternative partnerships; assess make vs. buy trade-offs; build capability internally if partners unavailable |
| Cost Structure Assumptions Weak | Benchmark against similar businesses; validate assumptions with operations team; plan for 20% cost variance |
| Profitability Timeline Unrealistic | Adjust growth assumptions; identify highest-leverage cost reductions; stress-test with more conservative projections |
